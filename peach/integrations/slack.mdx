## Connect Slack

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations > Slack**
2. Click **Connect** and authorize Acme in your Slack workspace
3. Choose which channel to receive notifications in

## What you'll get

- New article notifications
- Feedback alerts
- Weekly digest of popular articles
